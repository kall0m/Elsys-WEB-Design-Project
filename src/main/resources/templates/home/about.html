About Me

Hi there! My name is Kaloyan. Welcome to my blog for design, tasty food and healthy lifestyle. I am from Bulgaria and I am an ICT student at Fontys University of Applied Sciences. I have a strong interest in software development and user experience. I am passionate about drawing, cooking and healthy lifestyle. I feel intoxicated by possibilities.

Healthy Living

"It is health that is real wealth and not pieces of gold and silver." - Mahatma Gandhi

I love to live a healthy life. I eat 90% sugar-free, I don't drink, I don't smoke. I try to do sports at least twice a week. I have a diet infused with superfoods and vegetables. I try to avoid meat which I eat once or twice a week.

Hobbies

What I really enjoy doing is cooking! I love to make and eat boosting breakfasts, healthy lunches and dinners. My specialty is desserts. I like reading articles about healthcare and biohacking. I love to watch TV series in my free time!
